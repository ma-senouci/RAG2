Mohamed Abdelkrim Senouci is a Doctor Engineer in Computer Science, currently developing AI projects using Large Language Models (LLMs), both without frameworks and with frameworks such as OpenAI Agents SDK and CrewAI. 
He earned his Master’s degree from UPMC and his PhD from UPEC, and has worked as a lecturer and researcher at UPEC as well as a Research and Development Engineer in industry.
He holds several journal and conference publications, including Computer Networks, Ad Hoc Networks, Annales des Télécommunications, International Journal of Communication Systems, IEEE ICC, IEEE GLOBECOM, IEEE WCNC, IFIP/IEEE IM, and IEEE SaCoNet. 
His work includes developing a real health-monitoring testbed for medical applications and a platform for indoor surveillance wireless sensor networks, with approaches validated through simulation and experiments.
